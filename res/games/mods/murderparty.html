Murder Party


What is it ?

You might call it "A fair FFA, but funnier".

It starts like a FFA, but everybody gets one unique target to kill.

- If you kill your target, you gain a huge ressource bonus (voted when the game starts), and one point.

- If you kill your killer, you gain half the bonus, but no point.

- If you kill anyone else, you get a malus for ressource, and you lose one point.

- Once you kill your target, another one is given to all the players.

- The game ends when only one player left, after a final 1v1 fight - The winner is the one with the highest score. But you get extra points for being the last ACU standing.

- You can, of course make alliances ...

Play the 5th race of the supcom universe:

The Nomads


They don't have access to gating technology like the other factions seem to have. Instead they use space ships to move around the galaxy. On the battlefield they favor firepower and rate of fire of common weapons (rockets, missiles, generic unguided shells) over armor and health and other fancy stuff (beams, etc).

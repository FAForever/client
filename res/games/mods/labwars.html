LABWARS

You don't need anything else to win this war than LABs (light assault bots). Labs are powerfull, labs can shoot from transports, labs can own an ACU! This mod showcases the most powerful unit of the game, leading to very fun games!

Rules :

- All faction's ACUs will only be able to build Land facs, Air facs, Pgens and mexes.
- All faction's Engineers can do the same but can also build Hydro's and Radars.
- Each faction's Land fac will be able to build Labs, scouts, AA and engineers.
- Each faction's Air fac will be able to build Inties and Transports.
- ACU's can fire at air units with their main weapon, Overcharge is unchanged.
